- **Computer use in the CLI** — Claude can now open native apps, click through UI, test its own changes, and self-repair from the terminal (Pro/Max plans)

- **Vertex AI setup wizard** — guided onboarding for Google Cloud users
- **Improved tracing & LSP support**

## Claude Desktop

### Shipped this week

- **Claude Cowork — Generally Available** on macOS and Windows. Cowork brings Claude Code's agentic capabilities to knowledge work (not just coding), running locally in an isolated VM with access to local files and MCP integrations.
- **Cowork scheduling** — create and schedule recurring or on-demand tasks directly inside the Desktop app.
- **Usage analytics & OpenTelemetry support** for Cowork activity (Enterprise).
- **Group-based role management** — admins can organise users into groups (manually or via SCIM) and assign custom roles controlling which Claude capabilities each group can access, including toggling Cowork per team/department.
- **Plugin marketplace + admin controls** launched for Team and Enterprise plans.
- **Computer use** (Pro/Max) — give Claude access to your screen; no setup required.
- **Improved Microsoft Office add-ins** — Claude for Excel and Claude for PowerPoint now share full conversation context, support skills, and connect via LLM gateway.
- **Customise panel** — new unified section grouping skills, plugins, and connectors.

### Announced, not yet released

Nothing explicitly flagged as "coming soon" in this cycle, though the pace of Cowork rollout to lower-tier plans is still TBD.
